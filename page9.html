Explore Further

If the content of this website intrigues you, you might also be interested to learn more through these movie and book recommendations.

Spitfire (1942)

Spitfire aka The First of the Few is a biographical film about British aircraft designer/patriot R. J. Mitchell (1895-1937), whose Spitfire plays an crucial role in WWII. 

Twelve O'Clock High (1949)

Set in the early days of daylight bombing raids over Nazi Germany, General Frank Savage must leads a low morale American bomber group to fulfull difficult missions.

Flyboys: A True Story of Courage (2003)

The book recounts the Chichi-jima event and the history of the war in the air.  

A Higher Call (2012)                 

The book tells the incredible story of Charlie Brown and Franz Stigler, an American pilot and a German pilot that meet in the air.
